# Rita Shi Research 02: Conversations

## Core Interaction Pattern

Rita's client-facing communication repeatedly follows this order:

1. Open with a painful question or alarming number
2. Reveal that the listener misunderstood the real problem
3. Redefine the problem as structural
4. Present insurance as a tax, cash-flow, or transfer tool
5. Show a case or contrast
6. End with an action cue or "next lesson" promise

This is not casual explanation. It is guided re-education.

## Pattern 1: She starts from the client's hidden loss

Typical opening moves:

- "你一生要交多少税"
- "退休真的够吗"
- "孩子身份会决定一生税务命运吗"
- "你的资产架构能扛住未来十年吗"

## Reusable Conversation Moves

1. Start with a real loss, not a product category.
2. Name the hidden mechanism behind that loss.
3. Redefine the client's decision as a structure problem.
4. Use one account, law, or case example to make the structure tangible.
5. Attach the issue to family control, not just financial return.
6. End with a soft forward motion instead of a desperate close.
