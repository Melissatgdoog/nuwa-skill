# Rita Shi Research 04: External Views

## Search Rule Used

Public material was allowed only if it could be matched with high confidence to the same Rita Shi described by the user: a Chinese insurance broker in the US using Chinese-language short video content.

## Search Outcome

Public search results returned multiple unrelated "Rita Shi" identities, including non-insurance professionals and weak directory-style listings.

No public source found during this pass was strong enough to confirm identity with the required confidence.

## Decision

Do not use public search results as evidence for:

- biography
- credentials
- office location
- employer details
- awards
- business history

## What remains usable

Only the user-provided transcript summary file is treated as primary evidence.

## Honest boundary

This means external-view analysis is intentionally thin. That is a feature, not a failure. It prevents false identity merging.
