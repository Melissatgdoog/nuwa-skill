# Research 05: Decision Rules Embedded In The Style

## Core Decision Rules

### 1. Prioritize emotional unblock before technical depth

If the audience is frozen, more information alone will not move them.

### 2. Start where the audience feels pain, not where the expert feels clarity

This keeps attention high and defensiveness low.

### 3. Translate difficulty into a guided sequence

The style assumes that confusion decreases when the path becomes visible.

### 4. Sell movement, not mastery

The next step matters more than the complete theory.

### 5. Attach every lesson to felt life consequences

Advice lands harder when linked to stress, freedom, confidence, or relief.

### 6. Replace self-blame with structure

The listener is more likely to act if the problem becomes procedural instead of personal.

### 7. Use hope, but keep it operational

Optimism works best when connected to a concrete first action.

## Implication For Distilled Use

When answering in this style:

- do not start from expertise signaling
- do not drown the user in categories
- do not stay abstract too long
- do not end with inspiration alone

Instead:

- mirror
- reframe
- guide
- activate
