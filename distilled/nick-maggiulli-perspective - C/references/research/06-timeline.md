## Timeline

### 2017: Public writing system begins

- The About page says the blog began in early 2017 with a commitment to publish regularly.
- Early posts already show the basic traits:
  - simple financial questions
  - arithmetic that readers can follow
  - concrete examples
  - direct conclusions

### 2019-2020: Stronger "data over cliché" identity becomes visible

- Posts such as `The Biggest Lie in Personal Finance` and `The Most Important Number in Personal Finance` show a more confident pattern of:
  - challenging weak finance clichés
  - introducing cleaner comparisons
  - using data to qualify or reject popular advice

### 2022: The first book formalizes the research-first public positioning

- The `Just Keep Buying` page explicitly says the project was shaped by years of running numbers on personal-finance questions.
- This marks a clearer consolidation of the public communication system into book-length structure.

### 2023-2024: Broader consumer-finance comparison work

- Posts such as `Is 200k a Year Good?` show continued interest in making status-heavy or vague money questions measurable and comparable.
- The style becomes increasingly comfortable using percentile context, medians, and filtered comparisons to answer everyday money questions.

### 2025-2026: Larger wealth-structure and housing arguments

- Recent posts such as `Is Home Equity Fake Wealth?`, `How Not to Invest`, and housing-related essays show the same underlying method applied to larger macro and wealth-structure questions.
- The recurring output pattern remains stable:
  - start with a public debate
  - define the real question
  - compare the right things
  - keep the conclusion narrower than the headline

## What changed over time

The output appears to evolve in scale more than in basic method.

What stays stable:

- question-led openings
- low-drama analysis
- interpretable numbers
- context before verdict

What expands:

- topic range
- confidence in reframing public debates
- ability to connect household-finance questions to larger structural patterns

## What this file supports later

The final skill should feel like a mature version of this publishing pattern:

- simple entry point
- careful reframing
- a few useful figures
- bounded conclusion
